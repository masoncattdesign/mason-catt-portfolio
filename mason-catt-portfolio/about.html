What I believe

Design principles I live by

01
Systems over surfaces

The best product work isn't about individual screens — it's about building foundations that make every screen better. Invest in systems early.

02
Clarity is kindness

Confusing design is a form of disrespect. Every hierarchy decision, every label, every interaction should make the user's life easier, not harder.

03
Ship things

Perfect is the enemy of good, but "good enough" isn't good enough either. Find the sweet spot — then get it in front of real people.

"Knowing what you don't want is a big step towards getting what you do."
— something that stuck with me
