FAQ

Got questions?

Some basics answered here — reach out for anything else.

  • I start by getting obsessively clear on the problem — talking to users, reviewing data, understanding constraints. From there I move fast in low-fidelity to explore directions, then slow down as I add detail. I believe good design is iterative, not linear.

  • A team that cares about craft and ships things. I want to work on problems that matter to real people, with engineers and PMs who are genuinely curious about design. Company size is less important than culture and quality of work.

  • Occasionally, for the right projects. I'm most useful to teams that have a specific problem to solve — a new feature, a redesign, a design system audit. Send me a message and we can figure out if it's a good fit.

  • Figma is my main tool for everything from explorations to production specs. I also write a fair amount of HTML/CSS and have built Figma plugins. For research I use whatever fits the question — interviews, surveys, session recordings, analytics.

  • Success is when the design solves the actual problem — not just looks good in a mockup. I care about metrics like task completion, time-on-task, and error rates. But I also pay attention to qualitative signals: does this feel right to use?
